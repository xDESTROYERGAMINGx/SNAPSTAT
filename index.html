About the system

SnapStat makes paper data feel immediate, reviewable, and useful.

Built for real survey work—from preparing the questionnaire to checking individual answers and producing a report.

The scanner uses the printed form’s corner blocks, row markers, column markers, and calibration point to align the page and interpret shaded responses.
